Profile

Senior academic leader and legal scholar with over 15 years of experience in higher education administration, research and teaching. Currently serving as Associate Dean for Education and Academic Affairs at UPF Barcelona School of Management, overseeing 37 staff across programs and quality, international accreditation, student and career services, and global programs. Active researcher specialising in digital platform regulation, disinformation, AI governance, and communication rights, with publications in leading international outlets. Involved in active competitive grants from Erasmus+, UNESCO, NATO, and the European Commission worth over €550,000. Bilingual (English/Spanish); affiliated with Harvard Law School (2018–2022).
